Plumbum example

Simple plumbum views, not related to models.

This example shows how to add your own views to the plumbum interface. The views do not have to be associated to any of your models, and you can fill them with whatever content you want.

By adding custom views to the plumbum interface, they become accessible thouth the navbar at the top.
